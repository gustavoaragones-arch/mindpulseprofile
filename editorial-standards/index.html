Editorial Standards

Content Structure Principles

All long-form pages follow a standardized structure:

  • Direct answer summary
  • Behavioral breakdown
  • Structured comparison
  • Strengths and blind spots
  • Applied scenario

This ensures consistency and clarity.

No Clinical Claims

MindPulseProfile does not:

  • Provide diagnosis
  • Offer therapy
  • Claim scientific scoring
  • Assign medical labels

All content is educational.

Information Gain Requirement

Articles are written to provide applied behavioral insight rather than general personality description.

Each page must introduce scenario-based analysis or structured comparison.

Update Policy

Content is periodically reviewed for clarity, internal linking accuracy, and structural consistency.
